Australia at a Glance

Discover the land down under - a nation of diverse cultures, landscapes, and opportunities

Our Nation

Australia is a stable, democratic and culturally diverse nation with a highly skilled workforce and one of the strongest performing economies in the world.

With spectacular landscapes and a rich ancient culture, Australia is a land like no other. It is the earth's sixth-largest country in land area and is the only nation to govern an entire continent.

Population: 26 million
Area: 7.7 million km²
Capital: Canberra
Currency: Australian dollar (AUD)

Key Features

Unique Biodiversity

Home to more than one million species of plants and animals, many found nowhere else in the world

Strong Economy

World's 14th largest economy with strengths in mining, agriculture, education, and technology

Multicultural Society

Nearly 30% of Australians were born overseas, making us one of the world's most multicultural nations

Australia-US Relations

The Australia-United States alliance is our most important defense relationship. Formalized through the ANZUS Treaty in 1951, our alliance has been a cornerstone of regional security for over 70 years.

Our relationship extends beyond security cooperation to include strong trade and investment links, collaboration in science and technology, and deep people-to-people ties. The United States is Australia's most important economic partner and our largest two-way investment partner.
