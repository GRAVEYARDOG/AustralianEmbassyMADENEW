About the Embassy

The Australian Embassy represents Australia's interests abroad and provides consular assistance to Australian citizens overseas.

  1. Home
  2. About

Representing Australia Abroad

For over seven decades, the Australian Embassy has been dedicated to strengthening bilateral relations and providing essential services to Australian citizens overseas.

The Embassy promotes Australia's interests in key areas including trade, security, and cultural exchange. Our team works tirelessly to support Australians abroad and strengthen international relationships.

75,000 Australians Assisted
70 Years of Service
120 Staff Members

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do in service to Australia and its citizens

Service

Providing timely and professional assistance to all Australians abroad in times of need.

Diplomacy
